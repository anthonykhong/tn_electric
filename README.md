## [TN Electric Co. Ltd.]()

### Description:

  **_TN Electric Co. Ltd._** is a powerful and immersive Full-Stack Web Application designed to revolutionize the way you purchase solar panels and related accessories. By leveraging cutting-edge technologies like React, Node.js, Express.js, MongoDB, Mongoose, Tailwind CSS, and JavaScript, our platform offers a seamless and engaging experience for users seeking high-quality solar panels to harness the power of the sun and transition to clean, renewable energy sources.
<br />
Sign up now to browse which item suits you best or to request a quote!
<br /> 
This web application has been developed for a client with the aim of providing them with a powerful online platform for selling solar panels and related accessories.
